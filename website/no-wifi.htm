Installing without wireless

This is tedious, so avoid it if you can.
  1. Download the application package to a PC or Mac with iTunes
  2. Connect the iPad to the PC or Mac, and start iTunes
  3. Select Apps under Library in the left sidebar in iTunes, a page of app icons should appear on the right
  4. Drag the download from step 1 onto the app page in iTunes; or select File->Add to Library... and browse to the download/
  5. Start Park Observer, by tapping the icon on the iPad
  6. Download maps and protocols from the server
  7. In iTunes, click on your iPad under Devices in the left sidebar in iTunes
  8. In iTunes, click on Apps, in the list of options along the top of the iTunes window, you should see a list of apps on your device.
  9. Scroll down the list of apps, and select Park Observer.
  10. At the bottom right of the page is a button to add files to park observer. Click the button and select the maps and protocols downloaded in the previous step
  11. Open Park Observer on the iPad and tap Map or Survey to select and use the new files
  12. Consult the getting started screenshot for help.
