Getting Started without Wifi

These instructions are for those who cannot connect their iPad to the NPS wireless network. This process is a lot more tedious (and no better) than the over air solution. It is recommended that you only use this if you have no other option.

These instructions are also for those with custom basemaps or protocols that are not in the list of existing basemap or existing protocol. In this case, you only need to use installing basemaps or installing protocols as necessary. Contact us if you would like your custom basemap or protocol in the server list.

Installing the App

  1. Download the application package (v0.9.8b) to your computer. Previous versions can be found here.
  2. Connect your iPad to your computer with a USB cable. The iPad can only be synced with one computer at a time, so always try to use the same computer.
  3. Open iTunes on the computer.
  4. Click "Allow" in iTunes when prompted to allow access to the connected iPad.
  5. Click the "Trust" alert on your iPad.
  6. Select the iPad icon at the top left of the iTunes window to see the details of your iPad.
  7. Click on Apps in the list on the left of the iTunes window.
  8. Click the small icon at the very top left and select Add File to Library... (or press Control-O).
  9. Browse to and open the file downloaded in the first step.
  10. Click the Install button next to Park Observer in the list of Apps.
  11. Click on the Apply button at the bottom left of the iTunes window.
  12. Installation progress will be reported in the banner at the top of the iTunes window.
  13. When done, click the eject button next to the name of your iPad.
  14. Tap the Park Observer App icon on the iPad.
  15. An "Untrusted Enterprise Developer" alert will appear, tap Cancel.
  16. Tap on the Settings App icon and navigate to General -> Profiles&Device Management -> National Park Service and tap on Trust National Park Service.
  17. Press the Home button.
  18. Tap the Park Observer App icon on the iPad.
  19. Tap Allow when asked if Park Observer can access your location.

Installing Basemaps

  1. Download an existing basemap to your computer, or build your own with ArcMap (instructions).
  2. Follow steps 2-7 from Installing the App
  3. Scroll down to the File Sharing section at the bottom of the page.
  4. Click on Park Observer in the File Sharing list. The panel to the right shows the Park Observer files.
  5. Drag and Drop the maps from step one to the list of Park Observer files, or use the Add button to browse to you maps.
  6. Some maps are quite large and may take a few moments to copy onto the iPad, monitor the progress at the top of the iTunes window.
  7. When done, click the eject button next to the name of your iPad.
  8. Tap the Park Observer App icon on the iPad.
  9. Tap the Maps button at the lower left of the Park Observer screen.
  10. If your new map does not appear, refresh the map list by pulling down on the list (press on the list and drag your finger down before releasing.)
  11. To use a basemap, tap on the map in the list.

Installing Protocols

  1. Download an existing protocol to your computer, or build your own with a text editor (instructions).
  2. Follow steps 2-8 from installing basemaps
  3. Tap the Survey button at the bottom middle of the Park Observer screen. (The survey button may be labeled 'Select Survey' or with the name of the current survey.)
  4. A protocol is used to create a new survey, so tap the '+' icon at the top left of the survey list to see the list of protocols.
  5. If your new protocol does not appear, refresh the protocol list by pulling down on the list (press on the list and drag your finger down before releasing.)
  6. To use a protocol, tap on the protocol in the list, and a new survey will be created from the selected protocol.
