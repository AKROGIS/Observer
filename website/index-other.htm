Brought to you by the Alaska Regional Office GIS Team



Getting Started

To use Park Observer, you will need an iPad with at least iOS 7.

Is your iPad is on a NPS WiFi?

Yes: Use the browser on your iPad to go to http://akrgis.nps.gov/Observer, and follow the instructions on that page.

No: Contact you local IT to see if it is possible to get your iPad on NPS WiFi. If not, then follow these instructions.

I don't know: Use the browser on your iPad to go to http://akrgis.nps.gov/Observer. If you get a web page, then you are on NPS WiFi.
